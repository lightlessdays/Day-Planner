Privacy Policy for Wandr: Your Trusted Travel Companion

Last Updated: 21-01-2023

1. Information We Collect:

AI-Generated Itineraries: Wandr utilizes artificial intelligence to create personalized travel itineraries. These itineraries are AI-generated and do not involve the collection or storage of personally identifiable information.

2. Data Storage:

Limited Storage Duration: We respect your privacy, and therefore, Wandr does not store any data for more than 90 days. Our commitment is to ensure that your information is retained only for as long as necessary to enhance your travel experience and for research purposes.

3. Account Creation:

No Account Creation: Wandr does not require users to create accounts. We value simplicity and aim to provide you with a seamless travel experience without the need for personal account information.

4. Research Purposes:

AI Research and Enhancement: The AI-generated itineraries collected by Wandr are used for research purposes aimed at enhancing our artificial intelligence capabilities. This data is anonymized and aggregated to ensure that individual user identities are not disclosed or compromised.

5. Information Sharing:

Third-Party Sharing: Wandr does not share any personally identifiable information with third parties. The data collected for research purposes is aggregated and anonymized, ensuring that individual user privacy is maintained.

6. Security Measures:

Data Encryption: Wandr employs robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. All data transmissions within the app are encrypted to safeguard your privacy.

Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities, ensuring the ongoing integrity and confidentiality of your data.

7. Compliance with Laws:

Legal Compliance: Wandr complies with all applicable data protection and privacy laws. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of privacy and security.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy:

Updates: Wandr reserves the right to update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be communicated through the app or our official website. It is your responsibility to review the policy regularly to stay informed about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

9. Contact Us:

Questions or Concerns: If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy or Wandr's data practices, please contact us at contact@dhruvbadaya.in.

Thank you for choosing Wandr as your travel companion. Your trust is of utmost importance to us, and we are dedicated to providing you with a secure and enriching travel experience.
